How to use our service

1. Put the location

Login to your page and change your status to client.
After that, put the location you want to go for finding your errand man.

2. choose your errand man

You can see some people who are around the location you put.
So check about there and choose a person you ask him / her to go on errand and give more information to ask.

3. Check the estimated derivery time.

Your errand man will tell you the estimated derivery time.
You can check it on your page. Then you can just wait to the errand man arrives.

4. Evaluate the service

After you get item from your errand man, please evaluate his / her service from your page.
